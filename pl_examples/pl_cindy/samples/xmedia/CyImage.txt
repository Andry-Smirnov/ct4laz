The TcyImage component

Image component with zoom property and Control canvas access. 

Public properties
* CanvasControl: TCanvas
* MouseIsDown: Boolean
* MouseDownOffsetX: Integer 
* MouseDownOffsetY: Integer
* MouseDownFromPos: TPoint
* MouseDownFromPixel: TPoint

Published properties
* Color: TColor - Background color (Transparent must be false)
* MouseOptions: TcyImageMouseOptions
* Zoom: Float
* OffsetX: Integer
* OffsetY: Integer

Events 
* BeforePaint
* OnPaint
