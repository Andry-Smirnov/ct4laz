The TcyAdvPanel component

A Panel with gradient, multi-bevels, shadow and wallpaper features. 

Published properties
- Bevels: TcyBevels - Bevels
- CaptionOrientation: TCaptionOrientation
- Degrade: TcyGradient - gradient background rendering
- Layout: TTextLayout - vertical alignment
- RunTimeDesign: TcyRunTimeDesign - Moving/resizing feature at run-time
- Shadow: TcyShadowText - shadow text rendering
- Wallpaper: TcyBgPicture - image background
- WordWrap: Boolean

Events 
- OnMouseEnter
- OnMouseLeave
- OnPaint
- OnVisibleChanging
- OnStartRunTimeDesign
- OnDoRunTimeDesign
- OnEndRunTimeDesign
